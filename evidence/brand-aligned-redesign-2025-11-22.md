## 🎯 Design Principles Applied

### 1. Actual Brand Alignment
- ✅ Yellow (#FACC15) as primary
- ✅ Blue (#2563EB) as secondary
- ✅ Gray hero background
- ❌ Removed pink completely
- ❌ Removed multi-color gradients

### 2. Clear Section Distinctions
- **Gray** hero → **Yellow** wave → **White** about → **Blue** mission → **White** programs → **Gray** partners → **Yellow** CTA

- Transitions are smooth (wave shape, color changes)

### 3. Professional NGO Aesthetic
- Clean typography (not ultra-bold)

- Subtle animations (not flashy)

### 4. Horizontal Images (Not Portrait)
- All images 4:3 aspect ratio
- Show groups of people
- Documentary-style photography
- Full context visible

### 5. Micro-Animations & Transitions
- Image zoom on hover (700ms)
- Button scale effects (105%)
- Arrow slides (translateX)
- Grayscale → color transitions
- Card lift effects
- Pulsing background accents

### 6. Premium Feel
- Glassmorphism cards
- Backdrop blur effects
- Smooth gradients
- Shadow elevations
- Rounded corners (xl, 2xl)
- Professional spacing (py-24)
